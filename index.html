About Us

We Are Young Professionals

The YPF is a non-profit and inter-denominational initiative of Dr. Williams Folorunsho Kumuyi, the General Superintendent of the Deeper Christian Life Ministry. With over 1,500 members conceived out of his passion to help the youths of this generation discover the essence of purposeful living through diligence and hard work, honesty and integrity that will prepare them for excellence in their chosen fields of endeavour.

We are change agents

We are the generation that will stand for the right thing, no matter the implication, our integrity will be unshakable.

WEEKLY EVENTS

Sunday Meet-Up

Join our weekly professional forum discussion! Every Sunday, we connect in person with other professionals in our many locations for a stimulating and engaging discussion on a variety of professional topics centering but not limited to;

  • Career growth: How to advance your career, build your skills, and achieve your professional goals.
  • Job searching: How to find the right job, write a resume that gets noticed, and ace your job interviews.
  • Business development: How to grow your business, how to market your products or services, and how to build relationships with clients.
  • Marriage: How to plan and build a strong and healthy marriage, communicate effectively, and resolve conflict.
  • Tech advancement: How new technologies are changing the workplace, how to stay up-to-date on the latest trends, and how to use technology to improve your work.
  • Financial wealth creation: How to manage your money, invest wisely, and build financial security.
  • Family upbringing: How to raise happy and healthy children, set boundaries, and teach them valuable life skills.
  • Health: How to improve your physical and mental health, manage stress, and make healthy choices.
  • Creative topics: How to be more creative in your work, how to generate new ideas, and how to solve problems creatively.
  • Personal development: How to improve your personal skills, how to manage your time effectively, and how to set and achieve your goals.
  • Spiritual relevance: Above all, how to become a true friend of Jesus, stay grounded in your faith and remain righteous in your chosen profession despite living and working in a corrupt and corrupting society.

We also have a monthly combined meet-up were we can connect with more professionals from around the city. This is a great opportunity to learn and interact with other experts and professionals, exchange ideas, share your own brands, experiences, and build relationships that might just last a life time.

Whether you're looking to learn new skills, network with other professionals, or simply be inspired, we encourage you to join us.

Wednesday Prayer Drill

"Prayer is not a luxury. It's a necessity." - Charles Spurgeon

Every Wednesday, we have a power-packed, limitation-breaking prayer session. Prayers begin at 8pm sharp (GMT+1), so come early and get ready to experience the power of prayer. Every month has a divinely inspired theme.

This month's theme is "A New Sharp Threshing Instrument." We will be praying for God to, sharpen our spiritual strength and vision so we can break through all barriers on our way and see and cease opportunities for growth and transformation all around us.

Whether you are looking for healing, guidance, or simply a place to connect with God, we encourage you to join us for our prayer session. We believe that prayer is powerful, and we want to help you experience the power of prayer in your own life.
